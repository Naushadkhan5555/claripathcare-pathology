FAQ

Fasting required hota hai?
Kuch tests (jaise lipid profile, fasting sugar ,glucose pregnancy test etc) me fasting chahiye hoti hai. Booking ke baad team aapko clearly guide karegi.
Reports kaise milenge?
Reports WhatsApp / Email par share kiye jaate hain. Reports respective partner lab processing ke according milte hain, aur request par print copy bhi mil sakti hai.
Home collection safe hai?
Haan. Trained phlebotomist single‑use needles ,icu experienced staff aur hygiene protocol follow karte hain.
Booking kaise confirm hoti hai?
WhatsApp par details share karne ke baad slot confirm ho jayega. Aap “Select Tests” modal se multiple items bhi add kar sakte hain, aur hamari website se smart whatsaap booking feature use krke direct share karskte hai details.
